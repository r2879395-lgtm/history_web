Before generating the final timeline, you must define the scope by determining the total number of major tasks and sub-tasks required for the project's completion.

  • Estimate the complexity of the project (Low, Medium, High).
  • Define the major project milestones (e.g., Design Freeze, Beta Launch).
  • Calculate the number of sub-tasks needed for development.
